## 1. The Real Problems Schools Face (0:00–0:50)

### In simple words

- **"Who’s playing next?"** – Teachers and students keep asking. Fixtures are in one Excel file, updates in another, and someone’s WhatsApp has the latest version. Nobody is sure.
- **"Is this result correct?"** – Handwritten scorecards get lost or smudged. Someone types wrong numbers. Disputes and re-counts eat up time.
- **"Where’s the final table?"** – Results are scattered: some on paper, some in Excel, some in photos. Building a proper leaderboard takes hours.
- **"Parents keep calling"** – "What was my child’s score?" "Which match is tomorrow?" Coordinators spend more time answering calls than running the event.

### What we’re saying

- Running sports day or inter-house matches shouldn’t mean chaos, confusion, and last-minute fixes.
- Bharat Athlete is built to **solve these everyday problems** – so the school looks good, coordinators breathe easy, and students and parents get clear, fair information.

## 2. What Bharat Athlete Does – In One Sentence (0:50–1:10)

- **One place** for your competition: teams, fixtures, scores, and results.
- **Less paperwork**, less WhatsApp, less "who has the latest sheet?"
- **Correct scores and brackets** – the app follows the rules so you don’t have to remember every detail.
- **Everyone sees the same truth** – coordinators, teachers, students, and parents.

## 3. Features That Solve Real Problems (1:10–3:30)

### Create your competition in minutes

- Add your event name, dates, and academic year.
- Pick sports from a list: football, cricket, volleyball, athletics, kabaddi, and more.
- Create categories like "Boys Under 14", "Girls Volleyball", "Open 100m".
- **Problem solved:** No more maintaining multiple Excel files or different formats for each sport.

### Add teams the easy way

- Create teams one by one or add many at once (e.g. "Red House, Blue House, Green House").
- Link students to teams so names and classes appear on scorecards and results.
- **Problem solved:** No more typing the same names again and again or mixing up teams.

### Fixtures and brackets – done in one click

- Click "Generate bracket" and the app creates all matches and rounds.
- When you enter the winner of a match, the next fixture updates automatically.
- **Problem solved:** No more drawing brackets by hand or redoing them when a team drops out.

### Scorecards that know the sport

- Each sport has the right kind of scorecard (e.g. sets for volleyball, runs and wickets for cricket, goals for football).
- You just enter the numbers; the app checks that scores are valid (e.g. win-by-2 in volleyball).
- If something is wrong (e.g. 25–24 in a set that needs win-by-2), you get a clear message instead of a wrong result.
- **Problem solved:** Fewer mistakes, fewer arguments, and results everyone can trust.

### Live leaderboards and match results

- As soon as a match is finalized, the result shows on the bracket and in the leaderboard.
- You can see who’s leading in each category at a glance.
- **Problem solved:** No more "What’s the table?" – the answer is always up to date in one place.

### One login for coordinators and teachers

- School admins and coordinators manage competitions and enter scores.
- Teachers and coaches can be given access to enter scores for their matches.
- **Problem solved:** No more "only one person has the file" – the right people have access to the right things.

## 4. Future Features – What’s Coming (3:30–4:30)

*These are planned to make Bharat Athlete even more useful for schools, students, and parents.*

### Global ranking dashboard

- See how your school’s athletes and teams compare **across schools** (e.g. district or region).
- Students get motivated by seeing their rank among peers; schools get visibility for their sports program.
- **In simple words:** "Where do we stand?" – not just in your school, but in a bigger picture.

### Public webpage for your school’s competition

- A **dedicated webpage** for each of your events (e.g. "ABC School Sports Day 2025–26").
- **Students and parents** can open this link on their phone – no login required – and see:
  - **Live scores** as matches are updated.
  - **Current rankings** and leaderboards.
  - **Today’s fixtures** and "who plays next."
- No more hundreds of calls and messages; one link does the job.
- **In simple words:** "One link to share – everyone stays in the loop."

### Automated certificate generation

- When results are finalized, the app can **generate certificates** (e.g. 1st, 2nd, 3rd place, participation).
- Download or print in bulk instead of writing certificates by hand.
- **In simple words:** "Certificates ready in minutes, not days."

### Share results on social media (Instagram, Facebook, etc.)

- Students (or the school) can **share their result or achievement** directly to Instagram, Facebook, or other apps.
- One tap to share – "I came 1st in 100m" or "Our house won the volleyball final" – with a clean card or badge.
- **In simple words:** "Celebrate and share with one click – no copying links or taking screenshots."
